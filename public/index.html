Peer Politics

For our project we are tackling one powerful force in our current political climate: The Media. How do people perceive the media and how does their language affect our opinions and decisions? Peer Politics strives to open up your mind and allow you to reflect on yourself and your community.



Here's how the game works →

1. When you start the game, we will show you a headline without showing you the source, and YOU decide where you think that headline's source falls on the political spectrum.

2. Submit your vote!

3. See your results and compare against your peers.

4. Play again!
